FAQ's – Web Design Brisbane

How much does web design cost in Brisbane?

Most Brisbane small businesses invest between $1,500 and $5,000+ for a custom website, depending on page count, design complexity, copywriting, and how conversion-focused the build needs to be. At The MX Studio, pricing is straightforward: $1,500 for a 3-page site, then $250 per additional page. A solid 7-page website for a Brisbane small business typically starts around $2,500. Custom builds with multiple landing pages or advanced features sit higher.

Do Brisbane businesses really need a custom website?

Brisbane is a competitive market. Template websites from Wix or Squarespace can look decent, but they tend to be slow, hard to rank, and impossible to differentiate from every other local business using the same layout. A custom-coded website loads faster, scores better on Core Web Vitals, and gives you full control over structure and SEO. For Brisbane businesses serious about growing, it makes a real difference.

How do I get my Brisbane business to rank on Google?

Local SEO for Brisbane businesses comes down to three things: a technically sound website, optimised service pages targeting how Brisbane locals actually search, and a strong Google Business Profile. Every site we build includes a solid SEO foundation. Ongoing SEO goes further with keyword research, new content pages, and monthly optimisation.

How long does it take to build a website for my Brisbane business?

A typical Brisbane business website takes 2 to 6 weeks from kick-off to launch. Timeline depends on scope, how quickly we get content and feedback, and the complexity of any custom features. Smaller starter sites can launch faster. Larger authority builds with multiple landing pages and custom functionality take longer but are built to a higher standard throughout.
