who I am & what I do

I’m a front-end developer currently completing a Master’s in Interaction Design. I strive for clear logic, smooth interactions, and experiences that respect the person on the other side of the screen. My background spans interior design, architecture, and web design, so I naturally think in systems, spaces, and flows. I design and build responsive, hand-coded websites that feel refreshing to use and are visually considered, using my creative background to shape interfaces that actually capture and keep users’ attention. I’m drawn to how people move through environments, and I believe that same sense of spatial clarity should exist in digital products. Interfaces should feel like well-designed spaces: intuitive to navigate and easy to orient yourself in. I care about the details that make thix possible, so the final experience feels cohesive and not just good at a glance.

my work is...

  • grounded in UX research. Structures, flows, and decisions are shaped by real UX principles.
  • accessible. WCAG-aligned design, semantic markup, inclusive interactions, keyboard and assistive-tech friendly.
  • built for SEO. Thoughtful content hierarchy, clean semantics, performance awareness, and search-ready architecture.
  • responsive. My work works smoothly across devices without breaking at tablet widths.
