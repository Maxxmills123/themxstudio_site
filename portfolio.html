Design Proposal - Youth to the People

Youth to the People pop-up is a brand-immersive environment that translates their California skincare ethos into space. The proposal uses organic forms and breathable layouts to echo the “superfood, clean beauty” identity.

The layout balances product display with social, experiential zones, encouraging both discovery and community interaction. It frames the pop-up not as a store but as a temporary cultural presence that matches Gasworks’ creative energy.

Design Proposal - 'LATTICE' studio

The proposed commercial space for Haji Oh’s Lattice project is framed as a clean, contemporary environment that balances artistry with usability. The design emphasizes interweaving lines and latticed structures to create both visual rhythm and functional zoning.

The space is intended to draw attention to both the displayed works and the architectural frame itself, encouraging pause and interaction. Overall, it positions itself as a flexible yet striking platform for commerce and creative display.
