How much does SEO cost in Brisbane?

Our SEO services start from $950. A one-time SEO revamp covers the full technical foundation for $950, while ongoing SEO is $950 per month. If you also need website maintenance and hosting, we bundle both for $1,100 per month — see our pricing page for the full breakdown.

How long does SEO take to show results?

Timelines vary depending on how competitive your industry is, the current state of your site, and how much content needs to be built out. Most clients see meaningful movement within three to six months of consistent work.

What is included in the one-time SEO revamp?

The one-time revamp covers an extensive technical audit and fixes, meta title and description optimisation, page structure improvements, indexing and crawlability checks, schema markup implementation, and AI search optimisation.

What does ongoing SEO include?

Ongoing SEO includes everything in the one-time revamp, plus technical SEO monitoring and planning, new pages created to target strong keywords, Google Business Profile support, and monthly performance reporting and meetings.

Does SEO include AI search optimisation?

Yes. AI search optimisation (GEO) is included in both plans. We structure your site so tools like ChatGPT, Gemini, and Perplexity can better understand your business and surface it in AI-generated answers. Learn more on our AI search optimisation page.
